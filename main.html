Welcome to Mental Health!

This website intended to provide information about the effects of virtual schooling on students and help students manage their mental and physical health. Our website offers a student support bot that allows students to vocalize their frustrations and receive advice, a meditation exercise that guides students through deep breathing, and a health tracker that allows students to log their sleep, water intake, and exercise. There is also a list of external resources for both academic and mental health support.
